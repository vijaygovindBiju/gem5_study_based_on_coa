### Cache Performance: Key Findings (1, 2, 4, 8-Way)

*   **[1-Way (Direct)](direct.html):** Policy (LRU/FIFO) is irrelevant; 96.57% Hit Rate.
*   **[2-Way](2way.html):** Most significant jump; Miss Rate drops by ~50% (to 1.29% LRU).
*   **[4-Way](4way.html):** Optimal "sweet spot"; captures 99.54% Hit Rate.
*   **[8-Way](8way.html):** Diminishing returns; near-perfect 99.81% Hit Rate.

**Conclusions:**
1. **LRU > FIFO:** LRU consistently yields higher Hit Rates and lower AMAT/CPI.
2. **Associativity Matters:** Moving from 1-way to 2-way provides the largest performance boost.
3. **Efficiency:** 4-way LRU offers the best balance of complexity and performance.
